The Best Movies According to me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Adipurush

Adipurush is a screen adaptation of the Indian epic Ramayana that revolves around the triumph of Good over Evil. Raghav, the prince of the Ikshvaku dynasty from Kosala, has been serving a 14-year exile in the wilderness along with his wife Janaki and younger brother Shesh.

The Elephant Whisperers.

Bomman and Bellie, a couple in South India, devote their lives to caring for an orphaned baby elephant named Raghu, forging a family like no other that tests the barrier between the human and the animal world.
